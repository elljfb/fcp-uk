The program was originally created in response to the fact that currently only 6.7% of leadership positions in football are held by those with Black heritage. Through #Merky FC Football Careers<\/a>, Stormzy and adidas hope to address the lack of fair access and representation for Black individuals looking to build careers in the sport.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
