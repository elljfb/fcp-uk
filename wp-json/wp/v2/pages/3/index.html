FCP (“us”, “we”, or “our”) operates the FCP website (the “Service”).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1. Information We Collect<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We collect user analytics data via Google Analytics, which includes information such as IP address, location, device information, and browsing behavior.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2. How We Collect Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The information is collected automatically through the use of analytics tools, specifically Google Analytics.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3. How We Use Collected Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We use the collected personal information to monitor and analyze usage patterns on our website. This helps us improve the quality of our content and user experience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We do not share any personal information about users visiting our website with third parties.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

5. Data Security<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The personal information collected by Google Analytics is secured by Google’s security measures. We do not store or manage this information directly.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

6. Use of Cookies<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to track user interactions with our website.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

7. Google Analytics Rights<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The information collected by Google Analytics is subject to Google’s terms and policies. For more information about the rights you have regarding your data collected by Google Analytics, please refer to Google’s Privacy Policy.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

8. Children’s Information<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We do not knowingly collect or handle personal information from children under the age of 13. Our services are intended for a general audience.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

9. Contact Us<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the contact form on our website.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
